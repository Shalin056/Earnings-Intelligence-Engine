We also expect FX to transition from a slight headwind to ES revenue growth in fiscal 2025 to a modest tailwind in fiscal 2026.

We also expect that our average client funds balances will grow 2% to 3% in fiscal 2026. Putting all this together, we expect that our client funds interest revenue will increase $1.19 billion in fiscal 2025 to a range of $1.29 billion to $1.31 billion in fiscal 2026.
